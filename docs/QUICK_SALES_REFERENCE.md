## ROI Calculators (Quick Reference)

### For Solar Installers Licensing Platform

**Assumptions:**
- Current capacity: 30 quotes/month, 15% close rate = 4-5 installations/month
- Platform capacity: Unlimited quotes, 25% close rate
- New leads due to speed/automation: 100/month
- Additional closes: 10-15/month
- Average installation value: €6,000

**Calculation:**
- Additional monthly revenue: 12 installations × €6,000 = €72,000
- Platform cost: €20,000 setup + €1,500/month = €2,917/month (year 1 average)
- Net benefit: €72,000 - €2,917 = €69,083/month
- ROI: 2,370% (first year)

**Conservative estimate:** Even with only 5 additional installations/month = €30,000 extra revenue = 929% ROI

### For Homeowner Installing Solar

**15 kWp System Example:**
- Investment: €7,000 (after €10,200 grant)
- Annual income: €3,450
- Payback: 2.0 years
- 20-year income: €69,000
- Net profit: €62,000
- ROI: 886% over 20 years

- System production: 15 kWp × 1.5 MWh/kWp = 23 MWh/year

- Annual income: 23,000 kWh × €0.15 = €3,450
- System degradation: 0.5%/year factored in
