## Why LFP Batteries?

| Feature | LFP (Our Choice) | Standard Lithium |
|---------|------------------|------------------|
| **Safety** | Cannot catch fire | Fire risk exists |
| **Lifespan** | 6,000+ cycles | 3,000-4,000 cycles |
| **Temperature** | Stable in heat | Degrades in heat |
| **Warranty** | 10 years | 5-7 years |
| **Cost** | Moderate | Lower initial |

**Malta's hot climate makes LFP the only sensible choice.**
